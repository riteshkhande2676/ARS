Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about solar energy

Savings vary based on your energy consumption, system size, and local electricity rates. On average, our clients see 50-90% reduction in their electricity bills. With net metering, you can even earn credits by selling excess energy back to the grid.
Quality solar panels typically last 25-30 years or more. Most manufacturers provide a 25-year performance warranty. With proper maintenance, your solar system can continue generating clean energy for decades.
Yes! Solar panels can generate electricity even on cloudy days, though at reduced efficiency (typically 10-25% of peak capacity). Modern panels are designed to capture diffused sunlight effectively.
Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning (2-4 times per year) to remove dust and debris is recommended. We offer comprehensive O&M packages including monitoring, cleaning, and preventive maintenance.
For residential installations, the physical installation typically takes 1-3 days. The entire process from consultation to activation, including approvals and paperwork, usually takes 4-8 weeks depending on system size and regulatory requirements.
Net metering allows you to send excess solar energy back to the grid in exchange for credits on your electricity bill. When your panels produce more than you consume, the surplus is exported to the grid, effectively running your meter backwards.
